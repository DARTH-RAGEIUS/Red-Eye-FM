About Us

About Us

Red Eye FM is an online radio station, based in Nairobi, dedicated to playing euphoric tunes from a variety of genres and cultures, embracing the beauty of diversity in music. Teaming up with passionate music connoisseurs and DJs to curate phenomenal playlists for every mood guarantees our listeners get to hear the BEST of what the universe has to offer, making us THE home of euphoric music

At Red Eye FM, we recognize the underground creative scene as the lifeblood of the mainstream entertainment industry hence our initiative to recognise, appreciate and celebrate upcoming creators and their work; Fashion, Art, Music, Film, Tech, Writing, Dance…we cover it all. Through interviews, reviews, showcases and more, we aim to elevate talented individuals and their creations... Kenyan or otherwise.
